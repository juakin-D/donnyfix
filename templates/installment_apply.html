Installment Plan — Terms & Conditions

1. Eligibility

Applicants must be registered PhoneHub Ghana members, at least 18 years old, and provide a valid Ghana ID upon request. PhoneHub Ghana reserves the right to decline any application.

2. Plan Options & Fees

  • 2-Month Plan: 40% deposit, 2.5% one-time service fee on device price.
  • 3-Month Plan: 40% deposit, 5% one-time service fee on device price.
  • 6-Month Plan: 60% deposit, 10% one-time service fee on device price.

The service fee is added to the device price to calculate the total payable. It is non-refundable once the plan is activated.

3. Deposit

The deposit must be paid in full before the device is released. Deposits are non-refundable once the device has been collected. If the deposit is not received within 3 business days of plan approval, the application may be cancelled.

4. Monthly Payments

Payments are due on the same calendar date each month following the deposit. The monthly amount is fixed for the duration of your plan. Payments must be confirmed by contacting PhoneHub Ghana at 0541057500 with your transaction reference.

5. Late & Missed Payments

A payment is considered late if not received within 5 days of its due date. Two or more consecutive missed payments may result in plan suspension or default. PhoneHub Ghana will attempt to contact you before any action is taken.

6. Default & Repossession

If a plan is declared in default due to non-payment, PhoneHub Ghana reserves the right to reclaim the device. Any amounts already paid (excluding the deposit) may be refunded at PhoneHub Ghana's discretion, minus applicable charges.

7. Early Full Settlement

You may pay the full outstanding balance at any time with no early settlement penalty. Contact us to obtain your final settlement figure before making payment.

8. Device Ownership

The device remains the property of PhoneHub Ghana until the installment plan is completely and fully paid. Ownership transfers automatically upon receipt of the final payment.

9. Payment Methods

Accepted payment methods: MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money, and bank transfer to PhoneHub Ghana's designated account. Cash payments must be made in-store.

10. Plan Amendments

Plan terms (amount, duration, method) cannot be changed after activation. If you need to make changes, contact us at 0541057500 — amendments are at PhoneHub Ghana's sole discretion.

11. Contact & Dispute Resolution

For any issues, contact PhoneHub Ghana at 0541057500 or visit us at Osu Oxford Street, Accra. Disputes will be resolved in good faith; unresolved disputes are subject to Ghanaian law.

12. Acceptance

By submitting this application and ticking the agreement checkbox, you confirm that you have read, understood, and accepted these Terms & Conditions in full.
